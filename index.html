Tom's Paddle Journal

Here's my first time on the water. I used a recreational kayak provided by my buddy at a state park in central Virginia. I was in the boat for about an hour and we calmly paddled up and down the canal park once.

I was excited, so I kept visiting kayak retailers online and in stores. In the next few months I bought an 11ft recreational kayak from Dick's Sporting Goods. Then I purchased a second one so I could have the option to bring a pal.

Here's a newer boat I've owned since 2009. It's a world of difference from the boats I started in. It's nice to have for larger distances and longer days on the water.

I used it weekly while I was a college student. I put some serious miles on the green one.
