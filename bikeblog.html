My first time on the single track; I had a bike that would actually become unstable via the handlebar would wiggle loose no matter how tight it was fastened!
You'll hear me talking to myself in the video. By the time I was recording this trail ride, I may have gone a while without hearing a voice. It's gets quiet in the woods!

In Pocohontas State Park I was on the Lake View Trail when I flipped over a rock and landed on my back. Luckily, my camelbak water bladder saved me from injury and didn't even puncture!
I walked away with no serious damage, but unfortunately no one was there to see the epic wipeout. I remember saying outloud "Figures, no one is here to high-five." in the vacant woods. My bike took it like a champ.
